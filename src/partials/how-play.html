How to Play?

  • Solve Puzzles

    Match items to clear challenges and unlock new levels.

  • Strategic Moves

    Plan your moves wisely to meet the objectives and maximize rewards.

  • Collect Items

    Gather rare artifacts and resources to aid your quest.

  • Unlock Stories

    Each level advances the storyline, revealing more about Queenie's journey.

  • Compete with Friends

    Connect and compete for the highest scores in the kingdom.
